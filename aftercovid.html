After Covid-19

How did Covid-19 changed our lives?

Meeting up

People are asked to remain cautious around close contact, like hugging.

Leisure

People can go back to Covid-safe leisure activities

Travel

People can travel but with some requirements (PCR test, vaccines, quarantine, passenger locator form etc.)

Work

Working from home is encouraged

Events

People can attend events and parties but with some requirements (ex. social distancing)

School

Students can go back to school but extra caution is required

Religious offices

People can attend religious offices but with some requirements (ex. social distancing)
