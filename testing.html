  • 5 most important rules in corona times

    • Testing

    There are different tests you can get to check if you have coronavirus (COVID-19). The test you need depends on why you're getting tested.

    PCR tests

    • mainly for people with symptoms
    • they're sent to a lab to be checked

    Rapid lateral flow tests

    • only for people who do not have symptoms
    • they give a result in 30 minutes using a device similar to a pregnancy test
    Source: NHS

    Use the NHS test and trace website to find the nearest placet to get tested.
